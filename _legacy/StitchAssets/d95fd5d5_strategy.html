# Funduq Design Analysis & Proposal Based on the provided PRD, here is the design strategy for the **Funduq** luxury marketplace. ## 1. Brand Essence: "Premium Casual" The visual identity needs to balance high-end exclusivity with a warm, personal concierge feel. - **Palette:** White, Deep Charcoal, Gold/Beige accents. - **Typography:** Elegant sans-serif (e.g., *Outfit* for headers, *Inter* for UI). - **Imagery:** Large, high-resolution lifestyle and property photography. ## 2. Core Unique Selling Points (USPs) to Highlight - **0% Commission for Guests:** Unlike Airbnb, the guest pays no platform fees. - **Direct Owner Contact:** Transparency and direct contracting. - **Verified by Funduq:** Trust through professional inspections and photography. ## 3. Proposed Initial Screen Set (MVP Focus) To bring the MVP to life, I suggest we start with these 5 core screens: 1. **Home Page:** Hero search, featured UAE destinations (Palm Jumeirah, Saadiyat), and "How it works" for guests/hosts. 2. **Property Listings (Catalog):** Advanced filtering (Sea view, private pool) and a luxury grid layout. 3. **Property Detail (The "Star" Screen):** Immersive gallery, detailed amenities, and the "Request to Book" sticky sidebar. 4. **Host Dashboard (Overview):** A clean management interface for owners to see booking requests and revenue. 5. **Booking Request Flow (Guest):** A streamlined, trust-building flow for new guests (including passport verification step). --- **Next Steps:** I'm ready to start with the **Home Page** or the **Property Detail** view.
